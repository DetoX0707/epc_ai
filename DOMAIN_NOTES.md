# Domain Notes Used For The Upgrade

These notes guided the dummy data and UI emphasis.

- Uptime Institute describes Tier III data centers as concurrently maintainable facilities with redundant components and redundant distribution paths for power and cooling. This supports the app's focus on linked UPS, generator, cooling, EPMS, BMS, and commissioning evidence.
- Uptime Institute also frames Tier Certification around objective comparison of site infrastructure performance, which supports the app's emphasis on auditable evidence rather than generic answers.
- Recent data-center market coverage highlights that power infrastructure, transformers, switchgear, batteries, and other critical equipment can delay AI data-center delivery. This supports the Command Center focus on procurement gates, lead time, and equipment-level risk.
