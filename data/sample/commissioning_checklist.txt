COMMISSIONING CHECKLIST
Project: Mumbai Hyperscale DC-01
Document: CX-IST-CHECKLIST-DC01
Revision: A

INTEGRATED SYSTEM TEST IST-04
Scenario: Utility failure with critical IT load on UPS and generator recovery.
Acceptance Criteria: UPS shall carry critical IT load without interruption. Runtime at full load: 15 minutes minimum shall be demonstrated or verified through battery discharge calculation. Generator shall accept load and stabilize before UPS autonomy expires.
Evidence Required: EPMS waveform capture, UPS event log, generator controller log, BMS alarm timestamp, and witness signature.

INTEGRATED SYSTEM TEST IST-09
Scenario: Cooling tower cell failure during high ambient condition.
Acceptance Criteria: Remaining cooling tower cells shall maintain leaving water temperature: 32 C maximum at design wet bulb and approach temperature: 5 C maximum under simulated N+1 operation.
Evidence Required: Trend logs for condenser water supply, fan speed, VFD status, basin temperature, vibration switch status, and BMS alarms.

QUALITY HOLD POINT
Vendor submittal deviations affecting runtime, acoustic limits, thermal performance, control interface, or commissioning evidence shall be closed before equipment release for procurement.
