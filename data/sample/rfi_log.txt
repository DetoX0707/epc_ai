RFI-017 UPS AUTONOMY CLARIFICATION
Question: Can the UPS battery autonomy be reduced to 10 minutes where generator start sequence is below 60 seconds?
Response: Rejected. Tier III critical IT load shall retain runtime at full load: 15 minutes minimum. This requirement remains unchanged even where generator start time is below 60 seconds.
Status: Closed

RFI-026 COOLING TOWER ACOUSTIC EXCEPTION
Question: Vendor requests acceptance of 82 dB at 1 meter for cooling tower CT-04 due to fan delivery constraints.
Response: Rejected. Noise level: 78 dB maximum at 1 meter remains mandatory for all cooling tower cells near the east boundary. Vendor may propose acoustic attenuators without increasing operating weight beyond 18500 kg per cell.
Status: Closed

RFI-031 BACNET GATEWAY
Question: Can Modbus TCP be accepted in place of native BACnet for cooling tower controls?
Response: Conditional rejection. BACnet integration to the BMS is required. A gateway may be accepted only if it is included in the base package, factory-tested, and shown in the controls submittal.
Status: Open
