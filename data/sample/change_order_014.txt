CHANGE ORDER CO-014
Project: Mumbai Hyperscale DC-01
Package: Mechanical Cooling
Revision: Final

Scope Change
The east service yard acoustic boundary condition has been tightened after municipal review. Cooling tower cells CT-01 through CT-06 must comply with Noise level: 78 dB maximum at 1 meter. Any vendor proposal exceeding this limit shall include factory-installed attenuation as part of the base supply.

Commercial Note
No additional schedule relief is granted for acoustic compliance. Procurement release remains gated by quality manager approval of the compliant technical submittal.

Quality Instruction
The submittal reviewer shall cross-check design ambient temperature, leaving water temperature, approach temperature, fan motor power, noise level, operating weight, and BMS protocol against the master specification and RFI responses before approval.
